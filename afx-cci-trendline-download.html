The “AFX CCI TrendLine” draws all trendlines automatically, on the CCI indicator with special features.

  • ### Fully Customizable

Features

AFX CCI TrendLine Indicator draws the following on the CCI indicator:

  • Moving Average

    • Bollinger Bands
    • Trendlines
    • Channels
    • Linear Regression Channels

      Inputs

    ======CCI Settings======

    • Period of CCI

  • CCi Applied Price

  • Color of CCI

  • Line Width of CCI

  • Line Style of CCI

    • *Show Bars – *Calculated Bars

======Moving Average on CCI======

  • *Moving Average – *Show/Hide
  • Period of Moving Average
  • Shift of Moving Average
  • Method of Moving Average
  • Color of Moving Average
  • Line Width of Moving Average
  • Line Style of Moving Average

  • *Pop-Up Alert – *True/False

  • *Email Alert – *True/False

  • *Push Alert – *True/False

  • *Message for Cross-Up MA – *Custom Message

  • Message for Cross-Down MA – *Custom Message
    ======    Bollinger bands on CCI*======

    • *Bollinger Bands – *Show/Hide
  • Period of Bollinger Bands
  • Shift of Bollinger Bands
  • Deviation of Bollinger Bands

**
* Color of Upper Band
* Line Width of Upper Band
* Line Style of Upper Band

**
* Color of Median Band
* Line Width of Median Band
* Line Style of Median Band

**
* Color of Lower Band
* Line Width of Lower Band
* Line Style of Lower Band

*
* *Pop-Up Alert for Upper and Lower Bands – *True/False
* *Email Alert for Upper and Lower Bands – *True/False
* *Push Alert for Upper and Lower Bands – **True/False

  • *Message for Cross-Up Upper Band – *Custom Message
  • *Message for Cross-Down Upper Band – *Custom Message
  • *Message for Cross-Down Lower Band – *Custom Message
  • *Message for Cross-Up Lower Band – *Custom Message
  • *Pop-Up Alert for Median Band – *True/False
  • *Email Alert for Median Band – *True/False
  • *Push Alert for Median Band – *True/False
  • *Message for Cross-Up Median Band – *Custom Message

  • *Message for Cross-Down Median Band – *Custom Message

    ======Trend Lines====== * *trend – *To show or hide Trendlines

  • *Trend_Up – *To show or hide upward trendline (works if trend = true)
  • *Trend_Down – *To show or hide downward trendline (works if trend = true)
  • THighline – Color of the downward trendline

  • TLowline – Color of the upward trendline
    ======Channel Lines======

    • *ChannelLine – *To show or hide Channel lines
  • Highline – Color of the upper channel line

  • Lowline – Color of the lower channel line
    ======Linear Regression======

    • *Channel – *To show or hide Linear Regression Channel
  • ChannelH – Color of the upper channel
  • ChannelL – Color of the lower channel
  • *Regression – *True/False Regression
  • RayH – Continuous upper channel
  • *RayL – *Continuous lower channel
  • STDwidthH – Standard Deviation of upper channel (works if Regression = false)

  • STDwidthL – *Standard Deviation of lower channel (works if Regression = false)
    ======    Check in the back*======

    • *Back – *If you keep it equal to 0, it will check current bar. If you want to see if there was any trendline 5 bars back then you can set it to 5 and so on.
