This multi oscillator/indicator divergence Expert Advisor identifies when a divergence occurs between price and an oscillator/indicator. It identifies both regular/classical (trend reversal) and hidden (trend continuation) divergences. It has RSI, bollinger, stochastics, moving average, ADX, pin bar and engulfing filter options which makes it possible to filter out only the strongest setups.

Signal account (demo)

Due to the numerous amount of settings the Expert Advisor is *mainly aimed for the intermediate to advanced trader *who already has a deeper experience with divergence trading and risk management. I would like to see the main purpose of this EA as a tool for the trader who already have manually and successfully traded a divergence setup and would like to adopt in into the EA.

It is recommended to trade a portfolio of minimum 6 symbols at the same time to reduce risk as much as possible. Please also make sure that you first test the EA on a demo account and let at least 20 trades be done.

Whenever there is an available update of the EA *I recommend you to wait with updating until all open positions have been closed.*

### Features

  • Can detect divergences for the following oscillators/indicators: MACD, OsMA (Moving average of Oscillator), Stochastic, RSI, CCI, RVI, Awesome, ADX, Composite index, OBV and ATR. Up to 11 oscillators/indicators can be selected for concurrent divergences (occuring simultaneously) or any of 11 oscillators/indicators for multi divergence trading. It is also possible to trade single divergence using a specific oscillator/indicator.

    • Possibility to close half position selecting from different strategies.
    • 2 moving averages with different periods can be enabled as a trend filter. For instance MA 200 and MA 21.
    • Spread and time filters.
    • Possibility to use percentage of ADR (average daily range) for stop loss and take profit.
    • Averaging down function (also called as cost averaging). The EA complies with NFA FIFO regulations when using the correct settings. Please see the blog.
      Short description of the Composite Index which is one of the supported indicators: “The Composite Index was developed to solve the divergence failure problem in the RSI, but its ability to provide specific horizontal levels of support within the indicator adds to its value. The Composite Index takes the normalized formula of RSI and removes the normalization range restrictions. The formula for the Composite Index uses an embedded momentum calculation with a short-term RSI smoothed.”
