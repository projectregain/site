The trading robot opens trades regardless of whether it is a trend or a flat. Trades are opened under the condition of “breakout” or “repulse” of the price from the support or resistance levels. This robot has fixed Take Profit and floating Stop Loss. Take profit is set immediately at the opening of the transaction, and the Stop Loss is set in it in the main code of the program, but all transactions are necessarily with Take profit and Stop Loss. Stop Loss is removed specifically so that it is not displayed on the broker’s server, but only the entry and exit by Take Profit are visible. The stop Loss is only marked on your computer by a drawn line where the robot will exit if the price movement is in a negative direction. Another advantage is that this robot can trade quietly on all major pairs (EURUSD, GBPUSD, AUDUSD, NZDUSD, USDJPY, USDCHF, USDCAD), where only the minimum spread, because the take profit of 10 *points *(100* pips). Also suitable for trading in addition to the foreign exchange market – the market shares of all companies where the minimum spread. The robot works only on an hour interval *(recommended timeframe – H1). In the main code of the robot made all the settings and you only need to change the terminal, how many decimal places (for the calculation take EURUSD). Listing price 1,11523 is 5 digits, and quotation of 1.1152 is 4 digits. In the settings you add only zero “0” and change if you have a 5 digit system of quotations. All other calculations are done by the robot itself (multiplication of transactions, recalculation if negative SWAP, adding positions, Deposit calculation, setting take profit, closing by stop loss, recognition of transactions by a unique number). In the settings prescribed immediately trading lot/load on the Deposit and depends on the number of pairs, you just choose the trading load.
