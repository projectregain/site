The full description – The user guide for Pair Power Trading

This Expert Advisor is an One-click trading panel for multi pairs. It has the following functions:

  1. Display the power index of 28 currency pairs.
  2. Click OPEN button to open orders for the selected pairs.
  3. Click *CLOSE *button to close orders for the selected pairs.
  4. Click CLOSE button is to close the whole order ,instead of partial close. Close action has nothing to do with the number of lots.
  5. Automatically open the initial order and the grid order
  6. Close orders of one pair when the average price of the orders of this pair reaches the setting profit.
  7. Close orders of all pairs when the total profit reaches the persent of the balance.
  8. Close orders of all pairs when the total profit reaches the amount setted by user.
  9. Set the input Magic to 0, the EA will help to close manual orders.

  10. Set the input Magic to other EA’s magic, the EA will help to close those orders opened by other EAs.
    Regarding the currency power,please refer to the indicator Currency Relative Strength

    Usage:

  11. Select pairs as per the power index.

  12. Enter lots number for the selected pairs.
  13. In the power index, the positive is to open buy order,and the negative is to open sell order.
  14. Click OPEN to open an order for the selected pair.
  15. Click CLOSE to close the position for the selected pair.
  16. Check “AutoInitialBuy”,”AutoGridBuyWin”,”AutoGridBuyLoss”,etc., EA will automaticall open/close orders when the condition is met.

  2. On the trading panel, if the entered lots number is negative,in this case, the trade direction will be reversed.
