The inside bar formation is a bar that having its body and wicks entirely within the range of the previous yesterdays bar. The inside bar’s high lies lower and low is located higher than yesterdays bar. When these two bars form the pattern we are looking for it’s a strong signal for a potential entry. PA Inside Bar enter the market only with pending buy/sell stop orders with either timer expiration, OCO rule (one order cancels the other) or a combination of them both, stop loss and take profit.
