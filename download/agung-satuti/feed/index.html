Eazy Trade EA is the Unique Expert Advisor that trade 28 Pairs, and only open one position for each pair.

### How This EA Works

This EA will open new position for each pair, depend on the ea setting and close the position when hit SL or TP

Some position may have negative profit, but may be covered with other pair

### EA Setting

  • *EAName *= EazyTrade – Name of this EA

    • *DailyTarget *= EA will closed position when target reached
    • *OpenHour *= 5 – Open position in this hour
    • *CloseHour *= 17 – Close Position in this hour
    • CloseEndHour = EA will closed position on close hour
    • *TakeProfitTester *= 100 – Take Profit
    • *StopLossTester *= 40 – Stop Loss
    • *Lot = *Lot size for fixed lot
    • *MoneyManagement *= Money Management for lot setting
    • *RiskBalance *= Risk setting for Money Management
    • *Pips *= Pips filter
    • *EURUSD *= “5,23,50,30” – EA will open EURUSD in 5 Am with TakeProfit 50 and StopLoss 30 and close in 11 Pm
    • etc
      To maximize the EA Performance, you have to optimize this EA Everyday with 1 week history for each pair
