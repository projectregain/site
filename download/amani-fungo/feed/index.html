Take Profit and Stop Loss settings
To set your take profit and stop loss use fibonacci retracement tool

  1. For a buy trade, upon receiving signal alert draw a Fibonacci retracement from last higher high to a current point (Higher low) where you received trade alert, Place Take profit 1 at 100 level, Take profit 2 at 161.8 level, Take profit 3 at 261.8 level and Take profit 4 at 423.6 level. Though in Most cases (Take profit 2 and 3 will be hit much faster and for most trades) – rule of thumb, only the first signals after MACD have changed direction from Negative to Positive for best outcomes

    Now set your stop loss at the lower of the previous candle where you received notification

  2. For a Sell trade, upon receiving signal alert draw a Fibonacci retracement from last Lower Low to Higher Low point where you have received trade alert, Place Take profit 1 at 100 level, Take profit 2 at 161.8 level, Take profit 3 at 261.8 level and Take profit 4 at 423.6 level. Though in Most cases (Take profit 2 and 3 will be hit much faster and for most trades) – rule of thumb, only the first signals after MACD have changed direction from Negative to Positive for best outcomes

    Now set your stop loss immediately above high of the previous candle where you received notification
