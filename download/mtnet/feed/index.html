This is a simple panel EA for one-click trading.

* Trade from the CHART and trade from the KEYBOARD!*

You will able to open all types of pending order, and grids of pending orders and delete them.

Also, you can chose one of several types of closing orders.

For example, close only buy orders,only sell orders, only profitable or loss orders. 

You can also enable two types of trailing stops and breakeven, basket profit and loss,partial close with saving step of close to file.

*It’s better than save step to memory, because you will not loss step of close after restart Metatrader 4 or your PC. *

* * The EA distinguishes its orders from others, therefore, the magic number must be specified in the panel settings.

You will able to manage all orders by symbol or only selected magic numbers.

The EA save settings of Stoploss, Takeprofit, Lotsize and etc. after you close one. You needn’t to input them every time.

The panel is able to *take screenshots *when opening and closing deals to allow further analysis of the signals for opening/closing orders.

Stop loss and take profit are set in pips!

Do not forget to enable auto trading!

**To move panel on the chart, you should double click in the left upper corner of panel.**

### Panel Settings

  • ECN account – option for ECN accounts

    • Slippage – maximum slippage, pips
    • Show stoploss or % balance – show line with information about loss value in currency of deposit and percent of loss after close order by stoploss

    • Stoploss text color – select color of text with information about stoploss

    • Show takeprofit or % balance – show line with information about profit value in currency of deposit and percent of loss after close order by takeprofit

    • Takeprofit text color – select color of text with information about takeprofit

    • *Use make screen shorts – *take screenshots when opening/closing orders

    • Magic number – unique number to let the EA distinguish its own orders from the ones opened manually or by other EAs. If MagicNumber=0, the EA closes all orders on the current symbol
    • Use all magic numbers by current symbol – manage all orders by current symbol

    • Use addition magics by current symbol – manage orders by selected magic numbers (include orders, specified in field Magic number) and current symbol

    • *Addition magic numbers *-addition magic numbers, please use ‘,’ separator. For example : 111,222,5356 and etc.

    • Use hotkeys – Allow to use hotkeys, please see picture. Please be sure what chat window is active else hotkeys will no work!

    • X coordinate – position of the panel along the X axis

    • Y coordinate – position of the panel along the Y axis
    • TextColor – color of text on the panel
