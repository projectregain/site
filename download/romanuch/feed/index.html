Pair trading EA One trades according to the strategy of interconnection of two currency pairs, the so-called pair trading. The EA uses a unique algorithm for automatic learning on history. Now there is no need to carry out optimization and monitor the settings, the adviser will do everything himself once a day. You can use numerous bundles to organize the basket. You can download the startup settings in the discussion.
### EA Parameters

* Symbol1 - first symbol (does not consider prefix and suffix);

* Symbol2 – second symbol (does not consider prefix and suffix);
* Lots – lot;
* UseProfitClose – whether or not to use closing when profit is reached in the deposit currency, if false, closing occurs upon a return signal;
* Profit – profit in the deposit currency;
* AutoLearning – automatic training if the Range parameter is used false;
* Range – range of interconnection of instruments;
* DivideRange – dividing the range (default is 20);
* HourLearning – training time;
* NumberDayLearning – the number of days of history for training (360 by default);
* Magic – magic number;
* Slippage – slippage;
