What is Bollinger Trend PRO?

This is an advanced version of Bollinger Band and Moving Average. This indicator can be attached to any timeframe chart. There are three buttons on a chart to click.

If you click on the button “Bollinger on”, it will automatically add a Bollinger Band to chart and color the trends inside the Bollinger Band. When the trend is up, the inside color of the Bollinger Band turns green, when the trend is down, the inside color of the Bollinger Band turns red.

If you click on the button “Trend filter on”, it will automatically add a moving average line to chart and color the trends inside the moving average line. When the trend is up, the inside color of the moving average line turns green, when the trend is down, the inside color of the moving average line turns red.

If you click on the button “Glowing on”, it will automatically turn on/off the glowing feature of the moving average trend line.
