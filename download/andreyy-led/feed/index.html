The Calm trader Expert Advisor calculates price channels for periods M5 and M15. If both channels are directed up (buy) or down (sell), their inclination angle (M5_angle and M15_angle in the settings) confirms the stability of the trend, and the price is near the lower or upper limit at the specified distance (Open_sector), the EA opens a buy or sell order. If the order reaches a specified profit (Grid_interval), but the direction of the formed channels remains the same, the EA opens new orders.

The EA provides several Trailing Stop strategies (TS_strategy).

Deals are closed by SL and TP. SL and TP are set automatically depending on the formed H1 channel width.
