  1. TP & SL levels are calculated using average daily range volatility.

    1. TP & SL levels are calculated using Risk & Reward ratios 1:1, 1:2, and 1:3.

    There is a Trade Panel that will display account details and all the trade and signal information and statistics.     There are two additional filters to increase or decrease the amount and frequency of signals, the Signal Strength Period and the Volatility Percentage Filter.
