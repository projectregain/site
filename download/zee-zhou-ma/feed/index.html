The Mid Line Indicator is preferably used for placing limit orders at levels that are far from the market on M1 period.

Hence it is a great tool for risk management.

### Indicator Parameters

Percent

  • You can set a decimal percentage to indicate the level. 50% is the default.
    Look Back Period

    • Determine how far back in historical bars you want the Mid Line Indicator to look into.
      Zig Zag Parameters (Depth, Deviation, Backstep)

    • Set the parameters for the Zig Zag indicator used for Mid Line Indicator.

Global variables written

  • [ML][Symbol][Percent][Period] – It is the price of the Mid Line Indicator at current time.
