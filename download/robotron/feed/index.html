‌This indicator shows the trend by the selected period, and the set timeframe. On one graph, you can see trends from different timeframe (maximum 4 trends). You open trades in the direction of the trend on the border tunnel.

### Settings‌

  • *TimeFrame *– timeframe for a trend monitoring
    • Period – the number of candles for calculating trend
    • *Period start *– trend calculate from this candle
    • ShowTunnel – Shows or hides the tunnel
    • Method – two variants for calculating tunnel
  • Maximum – maximum range
    • Average – average range
  • *Price *– values of candles for the calculation of the tunnel
    • Tunnel % – 100% = calculated range of the tunnel
    • *PlasmaColor *– color of the main trend line
    • Single – plasma or a single line
