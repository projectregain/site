SafeMartin_VS_lite – the automatic trading system is optimized for EURUSD H4, but can work also at other currency pairs and timeframes. The adviser has a minimum of settings that allows to put him in work, without the long procedure of selection of optimum parameters at once. Work of several advisers in one terminal is allowed. It works as with 4 as 5 digit quotes.

The opening of warrants is carried out by the neural network using as entrance signals of the value of the Stochastic indicator. The neural network uses the reinforcement learning method therefore preliminary training in a tester of strategy is not obligatory though it is possible. For storing the current state of the neural network at shutdown, change of settings of the adviser, transfer on other terminals, all data automatically remain in the file.

For the safety of open positions are followed by a grid of warrants which can be disconnected in settings. There is an algorithm for calculating the step of opening orders of grid at large price gaps and big jumps of the price. The trading is allowed in both directions (Buy, Sell). Manual intervention is supported (closing of warrants manually if necessary). At this Take Profit of the remained warrants correctly are recalculated, the logic of work of opening of the subsequent orders is not broken.
