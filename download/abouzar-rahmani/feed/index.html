This indicator is working on 28 currency pairs. It is based on the multitimeframe strategy developed by Robert Miner. It gives a great overview of the entire Forex market. It shows values of MACD, Stochastic, DT Oscillator, CCI, ATR, RSI, ADX, two Moving Averages and signals in all timeframes. Imagine how your trading will improve when you can watch the entire market using a single indicator on your chart to pinpoint trends.

This indicator helps you to monitor all of the Forex market in one page and simultaneously track other eight indicators.

This indicator is working on EURUSD, EURCAD, EURCHF, EURJPY, EURNZD, EURGBP, EURAUD, XAUEUR, USDCAD, USDCHF, USDJPY, NZDUSD, GBPUSD, AUDUSD, CADCHF, AUDJPY, NZDJPY, CADJPY, CHFJPY, GBPJPY, AUDCHF, AUDCAD, GBPAUD, GBPCAD, GBPNZD, GBPCHF, NZDCHF, AUDNZD, NZDCAD, XAGUSD, USINDX, XAUUSD and one of your favorite chart (you can set it).

### Stochastic, DT Oscillator and MACD

The best Buy Signal happens when the values in all timeframes are green and BUY signal is ON.

Also, the best Sell Signal happens when the values in all timeframes are red and SELL signal is ON.

### CCI and RSI

The red color means saturation of a bullish movement (SELL) and the green color means saturation of a bearish movement (BUY).

This indicator shows two CCI and two RSI values.

### ATR

ATR signals are generated according to an original formula. When ATR value is green, it means ATR is in an appropriate situation for opening position. I have tested this kind of signal on several EAs and I have got positive results.

These signals are very strong!

### Moving Average

Moving averages are one of the other useful options in this indicator.

When the fast (shorter) moving average is more than slow (longer) moving average it shows BUY signal (green) and otherwise, it shows SELL signal (red).
