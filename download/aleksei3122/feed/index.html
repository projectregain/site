Copyist MS is a simple and easy to use trade copier. It works as a single-file Expert Advisor with toggled Master and Slave operation modes.

Orders can be copied from МТ5 to МТ5, from МТ5 to МТ4, from МТ4 to МТ5.

MetaTrader 5 demo version: https://www.mql5.com/en/market/product/28553

The demo version allows you to test the opening of market orders, orders will be copied with the lot of 5, some settings are hidden.

Copying orders to MetaTrader 4 requires the MetaTrader 4 version of the EA.

MetaTrader 4 version: https://www.mql5.com/en/market/product/25497

The current version of the adviser works only on HEDGING accounts.

### Parameters

  • Type of work – select the operation mode: Master or Slave.
    • Master account number – trading account number of the master terminal.
    • Copy Buy limit-Sell limit – copy Buy/Sell Limit orders.
    • Copy Buy stop-Sell stop – copy Buy/Sell Stop orders.
    • Copy Take Profit – copy Take Profit.
    • Copy Stop Loss – copy Stop Loss.
    • Take Profit (0-off) – take profit (0 – not used).
    • Stop Loss (0-off) – stop loss (0 – not used).
    • Profit transfer stop to breakeven (0-off) – profit level to activate breakeven (0 – not used).
    • Step breakeven (0-off) – breakeven step (0 – not used).
    • The trailing stop (0-off) – trailing (0 – not used).
    • Step trailing stop (0-off) – trailing step (0 – not used).
    • Add a suffix to the Slave orders – add suffix to slave orders.
    • Delete suffix in master orders – delete suffix of master orders.
    • Fixed lot – lot used in trading (if 0, then the lot is copied from the master terminal).
    • Order ID – identifier of orders.
    • Show info – display information on the chart.
