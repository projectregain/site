### Settings

  • *Main TF – *This is the timeframe that the EA will look for the best entries.

    • *Input 1 *– First Entry setup
    • *Input 2 *– Second Entry setup
    • *Input 3 *– Third Entry setup
    • Base_Direct – Trend filter

    • *Directional_Level *– Trend setup

    • *Risk Percent – *Risk for each trade based on stop loss size

    • *Partial ON – OFF – *Turn on and off the partial close function (recommended for experienced trades only)
    • *TP1 VS SL – *Point of the first partial close based on stop loss size. Ex: Stop loss size = 100 pips; TP1VS_SL = 1 ; First partial close will happen with 100 pips.
    • *TP2 VS SL – *Point of the second parial close. The same rule as above.
    • Partial 1 – *The percentual that will be closed when reaches the TP1 distance. *Related to ACTUAL LOT SIZE and not the Initial lote size.Ex: EURUSD 1.0 Buy; First partial with 100 pips. Partial 1 = 50; Lot 0.50 will be closed with 100 pips.
    • Partial 2 – *Same as Partial 1 and also related to *ACTUAL LOTE SIZE *and not the initial lote size. Ex: EURUSD 1.0 Buy; First partial with 100 pips. Partial 1 = 50; Lot 0.50 will be closed with 100 pips. TP2 VS SL = 2, Partial 2 = 50; When the price reached 200 pips (in case of 100 of initial stop loss) 0.25 will be closed as 50% of *REMAINING LOT SIZE.

    • *SL_TF – *Timeframe that will be considered to set the stop loss position before the lot calculation.

    • *SL_Range – *Number of candles to consider to set the best stop loss position.

    • *Trailling_TF – *TimeFrame considered to trail the stop loss. It will work since the start of the trade.
    • *Expiration – *Time of expiration in for non triggered pending orders .
    • *Spread On Off – *Spread filter
    • *Spread Value – *Spread value to filter
    • *Time_Filter_On_Off – *Turns on and off Time filter to open new trades
    • *Time_Filter_Start- *Time to begin to trade.
    • *Time_Filter_End – *Time to stop to open new trades.
