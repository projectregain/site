### PRESENTATION

This EA uses several non-directional strategies that allows it to generate profits regardless of price trends.

It manages a pool of positions (“session”) in a totally autonomous way

To avoid risks related to this strategy, a recovery function have been implemented (see below).

This makes it a reliable and secure EA, its DD remains low.

### RECOVERY MODE

This EA is designed to run on several pairs simultaneously on an account.

When the DD of a session exceeds a defined value (10% of the account per default), all positions of this session are hedged and the EA starts either a new session or a hedged grid. Its earnings will be used to reduce the initial positions. You can ask it to open a Grid in the direction of the trend (Automatic) or in the direction of your choice (Short, Long or Hedged).

This Recovery mode allows you to secure your account, critical positions are frozen until the situation returns to normal.

### DASHBOARD

Several graphical functions allow you to increase the profitability of this EA, for example,sometimes instead of an automatic hedging, you would like to cover a session when a resistance or support is broken, these features allow you to do so.

This mode is optional, this EA is designed to be fully autonomous

### VOLUME PROFILE

###

Another grid trading EA, you might say ?

Yes and No !

We all know that prices are in ranges 80% of the time, we also know that these ranges generate higher transaction volumes first because of their number and second because they are key levels.

The idea is to use the “Volume Profile” indicator to identify areas where many transactions have already been made and where the probability of prices remaining for some time is high.

### RECOVERY

Of course, as with all market neutral strategies, your account may be in danger if prices fall (or rise) indefinitely.

A hedging and recovery function has therefore been implemented.

When the DD exceeds a defined value (10% of the account by default), critical positions are hedged and the EA reduces them with its gains.

### PARAMETERS

  • General:*

    Magic number: the EA uses random magic numbers above the value 0xFFFFFFFF (4294967295).

    Grid volume: positions volume,

    Leg of the grid: do not use a leg that is too small, profits increase with the risks,

    Grid Take Profit: when the TP is reached, the EA closes all open positions and waits to find prices where a volume peak has been observed – do not use a take profit that is too high,

    Grid direction: direction of the Grid (Long, Short or Both),

    Range Size to exit at BE: minimum number of open positions to exit at Break Even (security),

    Dynamic Volume

Use Dynamic Volume according to the number of open positions

Step size of the Number of positions to increase the volume

  • Dynamic TP:*

    Instead of using a TP placed on the next pending order, the EA will close the positions during trend reversals, those reversals are determined by the crossing of moving averages in the indicated timeframe.

  • Recovery:*

    Max Draw Down before hedging (% account): level to start hedging and recovery functions,

    Unbalanced hedging coefficient: the EA hedges the positions + ‘x’ % to be in the right direction

    Proximity (%) to the top/bottom of the range: proximity to the top/bottom of the range (%) where a hedging position is opened

  • VolumeProfile:*

  • *Use Volume Profile: this function limits risks and increases profits,

    Level to identify high volume (%): a value of 60 means that all values above 60% of the maximum will be considered a high volume area,

Volume Profile History (Days): history used to calculate volume profile
