### Thresher scalping

The Thresher Scalping indicator gives signals by arrows in BUY or SELL indicating the likely price movement in the next 2-7 bars.
The signal acquisition algorithm is based on several combinations of a number of bars in history. That gives confirmation of the opening signal.
The indicator has several settings that need to be changed to get a better signal.
The best results can be obtained on almost all timeframes, so this indicator is suitable for scalping and trading in the medium term.
When trading, the stop loss level should be set immediately after the arrow. Profit can be closed on the third – fifth bar,
or turn on trailing stop.
The indicator does not change the previous readings of the signals, the arrow goes out and stands in the same place.
Thresher Scalping is suitable for many currency pairs, indices and commodities.
For each currency pair, index and product must be configured separately.
